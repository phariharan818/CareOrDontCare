Care Page

This page allows you to curate a personalized list of articles that interest you.

When you come across an article you find compelling on the homepage, you can access them here once you click the "Care" button. You'll see a list of these cared-for articles displayed below with relevant links for you to read at any time you want. If you change your mind about an article, just click "Dont' Care" on the toggle, click yes when prompted to, and and it will be removed from your list. Don't worry, you can always refresh the page to see any changes reflected instantly. It's your own curated space for the articles that matter most to you!
